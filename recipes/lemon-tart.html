Lemon Tart Recipe

An amazingly tasty and simple French Lemon Tart

The sweet summer is coming up and it's time to make amazing deserts for friends and family.

This simple Lemon Tart, made with love will take you just 75 minutes to make. You'll be able to cut 16 slices and delight all of your guests. Are you ready ?

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ¼ cup sifted confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons of fresh lemon juice, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons grated lemon peel
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 4 slices of lemon
  • 10 blueberries

Steps to make this delicious lemon tart

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking pan.
  2. Combine butter with 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar in a bowl until smooth; stir in 1 cup flour. Press dough into prepared baking pan; pierce crust with fork in several places to prevent crust from puffing up during baking.
  3. Bake crust in the preheated oven until slightly golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes. Let cool
  4. Whisk white sugar and eggs in a bowl; stir 3 tablespoons flour into mixture and add lemon juice, lemon peel, and salt. Whisk until thoroughly combined. Pour mixture into baked tart crust.
  5. Bake until filling is set, 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Let cool.
  7. Dust with 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar.
  8. Add the lemon slices and blueberries for decoration and sour/sweet taste.
